    • Zokos

    709 S. 19th Street, Philadelphia, PA, 19146

    Zokos applies the mechanics of crowdfunding to social gatherings. Unlike crowdfunding sites, your goal on zokos is a number of people, rather than dollars. When enough people commit, the party is on. Friends chip in cash so we can throw better parties. We call it friendfunding. When you RSVP, you can share the invitation with your friends, so we can meet new people in an intimate and unintimidating way.

    Technologies

    We use the Ruby on Rails framework hosted on Heroku for our website. We store our data in PostgreSQL and other graph-specific storage engines. We manage our code using git. A currently private API powers our website, while tried-and-true services such as New Relic and Sendgrid provide code and email analytics respectively. We combine external social graph data, internal graph data, and proprietary algorithms to help our users meet and make new friends over food. Our simplified interface makes hosting events and delegating responsibilities a breeze, while Wepay powers our event microfinancing transaction support.
