    Illadelph Sound, LLC

    Professional Audio Company specializing in Audio Solutions and DJ Entertainment with great Customer Service and attention to detail.

    DJ Services

    Illadelph Sound specializes in mixing the perfect music to compliment your event whether a Wedding, Corporate event or School function. We are passionate about getting people on the dance floor and making your event enjoyable and memorable. Our success comes from the lasting positive memories of your Celebration.

    Audio System Design & Integration

    We design Professional State of the Art Sound Systems for business. Whether you have a Retail, Entertainment or Office environment, illadelph Sound will design a customized Audio System to fit your needs. It is our goal at illadelph Sound to make sure that your experience from Design to Installation goes smoothly. We know that the Sound you experience will exceed your expectations.

    Live Sound & Speaker Rentals

    At illadelph Sound we provide our clients with two options when it comes to Live Sound. We can provide Sound reinforcement for private & Corporate events which includes a technician to setup and operate the equipment. As a second option we provide a selection of Speakers and Audio equipment for rental. Illadelph Sound is available to support events ranging from small Audio Visual presentations to large Corporate functions.

    DJ & Audio Education

    At illadelph Sound we provide DJ classes for those seeking to learn the basics of DJing and the Art of Mixing. The DJ concepts and Beat Mixing taught will culminate in a Mix CD completed by the student at the end of the course. Basic Audio concepts will also be taught to help the student understand the importance of quality Sound.
