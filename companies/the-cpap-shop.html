    • The CPAP Shop

    413 Bloomfield Drive Unit 1, West Berlin NJ, 08091

    Looking for a quality sleep apnea machine? The CPAP Shop has a store in New Jersey, and offers all items online as well. Our wide range of CPAP accessories include CPAP masks, CPAP machines, CPAP filters, CPAP supplies, CPAP nasal pillows, and other sleep apnea equipment. All of our CPAP accessories are from top manufacturers. Our staff will help you select the CPAP machines and CPAP masks that offer the best comfort, fit, and easy operation for your specific needs.
    Our CPAP supplies are available at competitive prices. CPAP supplies and cleaning supplies reduce the chance of infection with regular use. Delivering high quality CPAP accessories and sleep apnea equipment is the hallmark of our CPAP store since 2004.
     CPAP Masks: We offer a wide range of high quality CPAP masks with a 100% guaranteed return policy to ensure perfect fit and comfort to users. We offer all types of CPAP masks, including nasal, full-face, and nasal pillow CPAP masks from all major manufacturers. Our expert customer service representatives (who speak fluent English or Spanish) are here to provide advice on getting the best fitting CPAP masks for easier compliance with CPAP therapy.
     CPAP Machines: We offer high quality CPAP machines to treat sleep apnea. Whether you need a standard, auto, or bi-level CPAP machine, we can help you find the right machine to meet your needs. Newer CPAP machines offer many features such as portability, pressure and leak monitoring, data compliance, integrated humidifiers, and cost effectiveness.
     CPAP Humidifiers: We supply integrated, and standalone CPAP humidifiers. Humidifiers can be machine-specific or universal.
     CPAP Supplies: Get high quality CPAP supplies from the industry’s top manufacturers from The CPAP Shop. Our CPAP supplies include CPAP filters, CPAP tubing, CPAP headgear, CPAP chinstraps, CPAP cleaning supplies, tubing connectors, and adaptors. With CPAP cleaning products, minimize your risk of infection by making everyday cleaning less of a chore.
     Other CPAP Accessories: We offer a wide range of CPAP accessories including CPAP batteries, CPAP power cables/inverters, comfort accessories, Pulse Oximeters, Nebulizers and Concentrators.
     Portable Oxygen Concentrators and Nebulizers: We offer a variety of portable oxygen concentrators and portable nebulizers from top manufacturers like Respironics. Our portable nebulizers for asthma patients are conveniently small and lightweight. For true independence, many of our nebulizers are battery operated and typically run on rechargeable batteries. Our portable oxygen concentrators offer continuous airflow and pulse delivery in a single device that is easy for patients to use. Many of our portable oxygen concentrators weight ten pounds or less.
