    • Carpey Law

    100 West Elm Street Suite 310,Conshohocken, PA 19428

    Carpey Law is a Pennsylvania-based law firm focused on the representation of clients involved in complex civil litigation, including representing severely injured individuals in a vast array of personal injury cases. The specific types of cases we handle include:
    • Motor vehicle accidents of all kinds
    • Motorcycle accidents
    • Bus accidents
    • Truck accidents
    • Pedestrian accidents
    • Bicycle accidents
    • Fall down accidents
    • Work related injuries
    • Construction accidents
    • Medical malpractice
    • Traumatic spinal cord injuries
    • Wrongful death cases
    • Traumatic brain injuries
    • Insurance bad faith
    Attorney Stuart Carpey is a member of the Million Dollar Advocates Forum, whose membership is restricted to attorneys who have secured million-dollar settlements or verdicts. Mr. Carpey writes and lectures on personal injury matters, personal injury and insurance law. He is nationally known. He also volunteers his time and expertise for people who need legal services in and around the Philadelphia area. Mr. Carpey has also been appointed and regularly sits as a Philadelphia County Mediator in complex civil litigation matters, and as a Court appointed Judge Pro Tem in Philadelphia, and as a court appointed arbitrator in Philadelphia, Montgomery County, and the Federal courts.
