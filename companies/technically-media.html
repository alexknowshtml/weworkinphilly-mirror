    • Technically Media

    4040 Locust Street, Philadelphia, Pa.

    Technically Media Inc. is a Philadelphia-based media services consultancy that specializes in content and editorial strategy. The company's focus is a changing news ecosystem in which far many more, not fewer, organizations, groups and individuals will participate in news and information delivery.

    Technically Media publishes Technically Philly and is responsible for Philly Tech Week.
