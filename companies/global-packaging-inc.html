    • Global Packaging, Inc.

    209 Brower Avenue,Oaks , PA, 19456

    Global Packaging, Inc. is a quality wide-web flexographic printer and converter (bags, laminations, rollstock) and Film Extruder supplying high end packaging to the retail distribution channel. We specialize in printing complex HD graphics in up to 10 colors. Our highly experienced crews use state of the art equipment and techniques to provide consistency and quality throughout an entire production run, as well as from job to job. We operate in a lean manufacturing environment with a focus on quality and sustainability. As a certified Sustainable Green Printer, we work closely with our suppliers and customers to implement sustainability programs throughout the supply chain.
