    • Nissen Markers

    2544 Fairhill Avenue, Glenside, PA Glenside

    With more than 93 years of experience, Nissen is one of the largest manufactures of industrial markers worldwide. Since 1923, Nissen's product line has continuously expanded to include industrial markers for all purposes. Manufactured from the highest quality components, our products are designed to fill the needs of the most demanding professionals. We offer specialty paint markers, low chloride markers, solid paint markers, felt tip paint markers, and temperature indicating sticks for use in any marking application. Nissen guarantees all markers for the life of the product. Return any marker to us for a free replacement should that product fail to perform to your satisfaction.
