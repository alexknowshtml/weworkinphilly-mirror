    • Veneshe Master Venetian Plastering

    325 Stouts Valley Road, Easton, PA 18042

    Veneshe is the local resource for Venetian Plastering - we apply Venetian plaster; we sell Venetian plaster; and we train homeowners, decorators, designers, contractors and architects how to create and apply exclusive, old world Italian plaster finishes to walls and ceilings. Our Venetian plaster training studio is in Bucks County, PA and we travel throughout the area and nationally to apply and train in the US and in Italy. Our master plaster applicators were trained in Italy and can compliment our Venetian plaster finishes with decorative painting, fresco, trompe l'oeil, and even mosaics.

    The Team

    • Lori Blomstrom

      I apply, teach and sell natural Venetian plasters imported from Italy. As one of only a few manufacturer-certified female Venetian plaster applicators in Pennsylvania, and one of a handful of professional female artisans to have received advanced training and certification in Italy, I left a 18 year career at PPL Electrical Utilities to pursue my passion for reviving the art of Venetian Plaster, and I haven't looked back since. My work takes me throughout Philadelphia, the Philly burbs, Lehigh Valley and beyond; my Venetian plaster training studio is in Bucks County, PA and I also teach Venetian plaster courses in Italy.
