    • Leadnomics

    2929 Arch St, Philadelphia, PA 19104

    Leadnomics is an online marketing company located in the heart of Philadelphia. At our core, we're a team of talented individuals who bring a wealth of experience and passion to everything we undertake. Employees are encouraged to contribute meaningfully to all aspects of the business and to work creatively with colleagues to develop new ideas. We expect everyone to make a difference.

    Our office's open floor plan fosters creativity and collaboration. The Leadnomics kitchen is fully stocked with everything our team needs to stay fueled: Red Bull, a vast array of snacks, and an excellent selection of microbrews for those occasional evening code deployments. Our colleagues play as hard as they work, with regular company-sponsored social events like trips to Phillies games, summertime picnics, and beverage tastings.

    Thanks to the efforts of our stellar employees, Leadnomics was ranked #26 nationally and #1 in Philadelphia on the 2012 Inc. 500 list, an annual ranking of the nation's fastest-growing privately held companies.

    Developers:
    At Leadnomics, our tech is focused around transaction complexity, scale and management of big data. In short, the performance of our technology determines the performance of our business.  We often process more than 100,000 transaction requests per hour using our tech stack which includes Node.js, PHP, Java, Scala, Ruby, and various data stores, all on top of AWS with a service-oriented and message-driven architecture.  The dynamic markets in which we compete call for rapid response capabilities and continuous improvement to our platform. To accomplish this, we let our ideas drive our tool selection, rather than the reverse.  The development team is small, passionate, and nimble enough to learn and adopt new technologies very quickly to drive our company forward.
