    • WizeHive

    6 Poplar St, Conshohocken PA 19428

    WizeHive empowers people to get customized business apps up and running as easily as getting an app on a mobile device. These apps gather any type of data and get work done efficiently. Create forms, customize workflows and automate tasks without any programming knowledge. WizeHive business apps are secure, in the cloud and accessible through a social interface by anyone invited to share the data. Manage time by setting up and using WizeHive Apps for everything from timesheets to complex CRM systems.

    WizeHive makes it easy for business people to configure and deploy cloud based solutions that help them organize, automate and streamline their business activities. Solutions can run on a desktop or mobile device and can collect applications, run a hiring process, track projects, manage a sales process and much, much more.
