    • Here's My Chance

    121 S. 13th Street 4th Floor Philadelphia, PA 19107

    Here's My Chance (HMC) is a creative agency helping anyone in the world doing good things amplify their message and maximize impact. Our strategy is content. Our weapons are our minds.

    With clients like The City of Philadelphia, MS Society, Greater Philadelphia Cultural Alliance, Innovadium, Manna, Generocity, Say Yes To Education, The John Templeton Foundation, and many other Philadelphia based organizations, Here's My Chance has the following goals for every engagement:

    Grow passionate digital communities that fosters positive change.

    Develop meaningful supporter relationships through shared communication, mutual respect and grassroots inspiration.

    Run coordinated membership and fundraising strategies by keeping the technology simple and removing barriers to entry.

    Monitor metrics and analytics to ensure that all investments convert to measurable outcomes that furthers project goals (whether supporter growth, increased donations, higher visibility, etc).
