    History

    Established in 2013.

    We started in 2001 as a group of family and friends designing websites for small businesses, educational institutions and non-profit organizations. In 2013 we formed Biondo Creative.

    We want to be a disruptive force in the technology and design fields and be known for our ability to combine exceptional design, innovation and engineering to deliver outstanding solutions. Our goal is to be the leader in the creation of ingenious software products that appeal to people worldwide.

    At Biondo Creative our artist work closely with web programmers and cloud engineers to deliver a brilliant user experience through innovation, usability, scalability and cutting edge design.

    Today, we are a flourishing digital agency headquartered in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. We are home to an award winning team of designers, developers, engineers and expert consultants. We have more than 20 years of experience in digital marketing, web analytics, web design, web hosting and usability engineering.

    Meet the Business Owner
    Anthony J. Biondo Jr. is founder and CEO of Biondo Creative (biondocreative.com), a Philadelphia based Web Design and Digital Marketing Company that provides Web Services, Social Media Management, Paid Advertising and Digital Marketing Consulting.

    Anthony is a graduate of the La Salle University and obtained his Masters from Saint Joseph's University. His work in Digital Marketing has been featured on local Philadelphia ABC, NBC, and CBS affiliates and NPR and he has received awards from the Web Marketing Association, PRSA Philadelphia, IABC/PRSA Kentucky for excellence in web design and Dale Carnegie for Innovation.
