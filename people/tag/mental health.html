    • Summit Behavioral Healthcare

      Summit Behavioral Healthcare is a treatment provider for individuals battling substance abuse issues. Summit owns a network of addiction treatment centers in five states and is headquartered in Tennessee. Each treatment center offers a full continuum of care to ensure that their patients reach full recovery from drug and alcohol abuse.

    • The Ranch at Dove Tree

      The Ranch at Dove Tree is a drug treatment center in Texas offering both residential and intensive outpatient programs. The Ranch at Dove Tree specializes in collegiate recovery but also offers programs for the general population and owns an outpatient treatment center in downtown Lubbock, Texas. The company is accredited by the Joint Commission (JCAHO) and was acquired by Summit Behavioral Healthcare in 2015.

    • Mountain Laurel Recovery Center

      Mountain Laurel Recovery Center is a Pennsylvania based drug treatment center offering both outpatient and inpatient treatment services. The company was acquired by Summit Behavioral Healthcare in 2015 after operating as Seabrook West for many years. Mountain Laurel also offers transitional living and a wellness program for individuals in recovery from drug and alcohol abuse.

    • Valley Recovery Center at Fresno

      Valley Recovery Center at Fresno is a California based drug treatment center offering PHP and IOP treatment programs. The company is owned and operated by Summit Behavioral Healthcare and has been in operation since 2015. Valley Fresno is ideal for individuals that may not have the time or insurance coverage to pursue traditional residential treatment for their addiction.

    • Twin Lakes Recovery Center

      Twin Lakes Recovery Center is a Georgia based drug treatment center offering both inpatient and outpatient treatment services. The company has been in operation since 2015 after being founded by Summit Behavioral Healthcare, an addiction treatment provider in Tennessee. Twin Lakes also owns and operates an outpatient treatment center in nearby Gainesville, Georgia that offers PHP and IOP programs for its patients.

    • Victory Addiction Recovery Center

      Victory Addiction Recovery Center is a Louisiana based drug treatment center offering both outpatient and inpatient treatment services. The company has been in operation since 2013 before being acquired by Summit Behavioral Healthcare. Melanie Courville currently oversees the companies day to day operations.

    • Valley Recovery Center

      Valley Recovery Center of California is a California based drug treatment center offering both inpatient and outpatient treatment services. The company is owned and operated by Summit Behavioral Healthcare and holds accreditation with the Joint Commission.

    • Great Oaks Recovery Center

      Great Oaks Recovery Center is a Texas based drug treatment center offering both inpatient and outpatient treatment services. Great Oaks has been in operation since 2014 and was founded by Summit Behavioral Healthcare. The company holds accreditation with the Joint Commission and the NAATP.
