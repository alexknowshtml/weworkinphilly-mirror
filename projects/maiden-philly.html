    • Maiden Philly

    In an ever shrinking world, where everyone else is focused on globalization and turning customers into commodities, Maiden Philly is focused on protecting the culture and individuality found here in the City of Brotherly Love. Maiden Philly is an online store featuring the best of what Philadelphia has to offer. Our products are made, designed, restored, or otherwise created right here in the Delaware Valley. These products aren't made by faceless companies but by neighbors, friends, and family. Their businesses keep our culture and our city alive.
    Maiden Philly is an homage to our creators, to the men and women who make this city whole, who make this city unique, who make this city ours.
