    • Startup Weekend Health

    Invitation for engineers with programming or big data knowledge who would be interested in Startup Weekend (feb 15,16,17). The biggest reasons for developers to come are:

    (a) exposure to clinicians-- we have lots of clinicians as participants and the CIO of Penn Medicine is giving the keynote.
    (b) opportunity to live the life of an entrepreneur for a weekend with abundant help from high level mentors;
    (c) the possibility of forming a real company with the connections/prototypes developed during the Weekend;
    (d) all you can eat and drink (beer included) from friday to sunday
    (e) remarkably, a 50-50 gender ratio.

    The winner gets fast-tracked into the final round of interviews for DreamIt Ventures Health class enrolling this spring.

    http://healthphl.startupweekend.org
