    • Lokadot

    Lokadot is a free iPhone app that streams location-based audio to you based on what interests you, where you are, and what's nearby. It's a powerful way for non-profit arts, cultural, historical and tourism-related organizations to inform and engage with listeners. For businesses, it's a cutting-edge, uniquely effective way to build brand identity, attract new customers, and drive sales. And of course for listeners, Lokadot is an easy, enjoyable way to discover all kinds of cool new stuff that otherwise might have just passed on by. As an added bonus, you can use Lokadot right alongside your favorite iTunes music for a completely seamless, hands-off, serendipitous listening experience.
