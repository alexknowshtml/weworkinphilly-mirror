    • Tribe 12

    201 S. Camac St., Philadelphia PA 19107

    Tribe 12 is a network of intertwined projects that offer a holistic approach to 20s and 30s Jewish engagement. Our approach empowers young Jewish adults, who identify as Jewish in any way, in choosing a Jewish community or network today, leading to a life-long, personally significant Jewish connection. Programming includes an accelerator fellowship, co-working, LGBTQ programming, a Jewish learning festival, sports teams, and social, cultural and social justice events.
