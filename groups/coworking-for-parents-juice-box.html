    • Coworking for Parents (Juice Box)

    Manayunk

    We are parents who usually work from home full or part time, and who live in or west of Philadelphia (Mt Airy, Main Line, Manayunk, Conshohocken, King of Prussia, etc). We get together for coworking sessions regularly, and are working towards opening a permanent coworking office space (to be called "Juice Box") with on-site child care and many more services... to help us all find community, socialize, learn, get stuff done. . . and achieve balance with our work and home life. Benefits will include conference and meeting rooms, a nursing/pumping room, community dinners, space for parents to spend time with their kids on breaks, mentoring and learning events, and so much more.
