    • Philadelphia Flash Platform Adobe User Group

    The Philadelphia Flash Platform AUG (an official Adobe User Group) was formed in order to provide a common ground for the local Flash Platform design and development community. Our group provides coverage on the growing set of tools and applications for working with and creating Flash content for web, desktop and mobile devices, as well as many open source tools and projects.

    Through the group, members can exchange ideas, knowledge and information in a creative and supporting environment, advancing the current state of interactive technologies. As an informal, member-supported organization, we rely entirely on volunteers to help with meeting content, speakers, meeting locations, equipment and membership recruitment. We also have corporate sponsors such as Adobe and several book publishers like O'Reilly, New Riders, Peachpit, Wiley and Friends of Ed, that provide software, books, discounts, and other valuable donations to share with members of the group. To become a member, simply use the signup form. Anyone may attend a meeting, there are no fees or dues.
